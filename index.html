Key Recommendations
Start with Paper Circuits (Week 1): All 15 kits let the whole class participate simultaneously — zero prerequisites, maximum engagement.
Use Humanoid Robot & Tank as inspiration: Demo these on Day 1 to show students what they're working toward, then have them program responses mid-program.
3D Printer = design thinking layer: Use TinkerCAD (free) for design, print custom chassis and gripper parts. Turns building into real engineering.
Small purchases to unlock Phase 3: ESP32 WiFi modules (~₹300 each), extra servo motors (~₹500 each), colour/material sensor (~₹400) open up IoT, robot arms, and trash sorting.
